\chapter{Classical Sculptures}
\section{Starting position}

\section{\emph{Rapturous Reach} and the first three tethers}
Eden uses \emph{Rapturous Reach}, covering one half of the arena.  Everybody moves to the safe side, with the players with the \textbf{1} marker aiming the gaze of the statues away from the other six players, then falling back into the group once the laser fires.  The second and third marked players step out, aim the laser and fall back in turn.

\section{Final tether, knockback and stocked attack}
The giant Eden on the south side of the arena will hit either the southwest or southeast side of the arena immediately after the laser is fired at the fourth marked players.  The other six players wait near the impact point to be knocked back.

After the knockback, Eden uses her stocked combination attack from the beginning of the phase, completing the phase.
